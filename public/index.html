Appointment Guidelines

  • Pending: Appointment is booked and awaiting professor's confirmation.
  • Confirmed: Professor has approved your slot. Attend at the scheduled time.
  • Cancelled: Appointment has been cancelled by you or the professor. The slot becomes available again.
  • Rescheduling: When you reschedule, your appointment resets to Pending for professor approval.
